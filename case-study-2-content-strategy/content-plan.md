## Objective

Build a 3-month content roadmap to increase visibility for mid-funnel keywords related to productivity and automation, targeting small business owners in the consideration phase.

## On-Page Optimization Checklist

For each content piece:

- [ ] Primary keyword in title tag (front-loaded)
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Secondary keywords in H2s
- [ ] Meta description with keyword + CTA (150-160 chars)
- [ ] URL slug is clean and keyword-rich
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Internal links to/from related content (3-5 per page)
- [ ] Schema markup implemented (Article, FAQPage, HowTo)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting (short paragraphs, bullets)
- [ ] Clear CTA above the fold

---

## Sitemap Fixes

See [sitemap-analysis.md](./sitemap-analysis.md) for full details.

**Issues Found:**
- 3 outdated URLs returning 404s
- Blog index page missing from sitemap
- Parameterized URLs incorrectly included

**Actions Taken:**
- Removed dead URLs from sitemap
- Added `/blog/` index page
- Excluded `?sort=` and `?filter=` parameters
- Submitted updated sitemap to GSC
