# Technical Audit Report: The Morning Brew Coffee Shop

**Audit Date:** December 2024  
**Auditor:** Sage Nwanne  
**Audit Type:** Local SEO Foundation Assessment

---

## Executive Summary

The Morning Brew Coffee Shop is a high-performing local business with excellent customer reviews (4.9★, 432 reviews) but **zero digital SEO infrastructure**. This audit identifies critical gaps preventing online discoverability and outlines a remediation roadmap.

**Current State:** No website, no indexed content, no structured data, relying entirely on Google Business Profile and Facebook.

**Opportunity:** Significant untapped search demand for local coffee-related queries in the East Stroudsburg market.

## Identified Issues (Priority Order)

### Critical (P0)

| Issue | Impact | Recommendation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| No website | Cannot rank for any search queries | Build SEO-optimized website |
| No indexed pages | Zero organic visibility | Create 15-20 optimized pages |
| No online ordering | Missing revenue stream | Integrate ordering system |
| No structured data | No rich snippets in search | Implement LocalBusiness, Menu schema |

### High (P1)

| Issue | Impact | Recommendation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| GMB missing website link | Reduces click-through | Link website once built |
| No Google Posts | Missed engagement opportunity | Weekly post schedule |
| Limited professional photos | Poor visual appeal | Professional photography |
| No secondary GMB categories | Reduced discoverability | Add "Cafe", "Breakfast restaurant" |

### Medium (P2)

| Issue | Impact | Recommendation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| No customer data capture | Can't retarget or nurture | Loyalty program with email/SMS |
| Single-platform dependency | Risk if Facebook changes | Diversify to Instagram, Google |
| No review response strategy | Missed relationship building | Respond to all reviews |
