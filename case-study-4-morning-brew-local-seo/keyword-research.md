# Keyword Research: The Morning Brew Coffee Shop

**Location:** East Stroudsburg, PA (Poconos Region)  
**Research Date:** December 2024

---

## Keyword Strategy Overview

For a local coffee shop with no current web presence, we focus on:

1. **Local intent keywords** — "coffee near me", "cafe east stroudsburg"
2. **Product keywords** — "iced latte", "espresso drinks", "vegan pastries"
3. **Occasion keywords** — "breakfast near me", "study cafe", "coffee shop open now"
