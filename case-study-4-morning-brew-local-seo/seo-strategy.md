# 6-Month SEO Strategy: The Morning Brew Coffee Shop

**Client:** The Morning Brew Coffee Shop  
**Location:** East Stroudsburg, PA  
**Goal:** Build complete digital presence and achieve local search visibility

---

## Strategic Objectives

| Objective | Target | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Launch SEO-optimized website | 15-20 indexed pages | Month 1-2 |
| Rank for primary local keywords | Top 10 for 10+ keywords | Month 3-4 |
| Launch online ordering | Active revenue channel | Month 2 |
| Build email/SMS list | 500-1,000 contacts | Month 3-4 |
| Establish content rhythm | 2-4 posts/month | Month 3+ |

---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Slow indexing | Submit sitemap, request indexing, internal linking |
| Low conversion | A/B test CTAs, simplify order flow |
| Competitor response | Focus on review advantage, unique content |
| Technical issues | Lighthouse CI, regular audits |
