# Technical Audit Report: Moon Poke, Sushi & Sweets

**Audit Date:** December 2024  
**Auditor:** Sage Nwanne  
**Audit Type:** Restaurant SEO & Platform Assessment

---

## Executive Summary

Moon Poke has a perfect 5.0-star rating and quality product, but their digital infrastructure is **template-based and platform-dependent**. This audit identifies technical SEO gaps and platform dependencies that limit growth and margin.

**Current State:** Generic website, third-party ordering (MealKeyWay), minimal SEO, no structured data.

**Opportunity:** Significant margin improvement through first-party ordering and organic traffic growth through proper SEO.

## Technical Issues Identified

### Critical (P0)

| Issue | Impact | Recommendation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| No structured data | No rich snippets, poor discoverability | Implement Restaurant + Menu schema |
| Third-party ordering | 15-20% margin loss per order | Migrate to first-party system |
| Thin content | Poor keyword targeting | Create optimized menu/about pages |
| No customer data | Can't retarget or nurture | Capture emails at checkout |

### High (P1)

| Issue | Impact | Recommendation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| Slow page speed | Poor UX, lower rankings | Optimize images, reduce bloat |
| Generic meta tags | Low CTR in search | Write compelling meta descriptions |
| No blog/content | Missing long-tail keywords | Create content strategy |
| Limited GMB activity | Missed engagement | Weekly Google Posts |

### Medium (P2)

| Issue | Impact | Recommendation |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| No social presence | Missed brand building | Instagram/TikTok strategy |
| No review strategy | Slower review growth | Implement review requests |
| No loyalty program | Lower retention | Points-based rewards |
