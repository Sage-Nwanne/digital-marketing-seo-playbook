# Keyword Research: Moon Poke, Sushi & Sweets

**Location:** East Stroudsburg, PA  
**Research Date:** December 2024

---

## Keyword Strategy Overview

For a poke/sushi restaurant with an existing but underoptimized website, we focus on:

1. **Local intent keywords** — "poke near me", "sushi east stroudsburg"
2. **Product keywords** — "poke bowl", "build your own bowl", "boba tea"
3. **Health/lifestyle keywords** — "healthy lunch near me", "fresh fish bowl"

## Content Recommendations by Keyword Cluster

### Cluster 1: Core Pages
- Homepage (poke near me, sushi east stroudsburg)
- About Us (fresh, healthy, quality story)
- Hours & Location

### Cluster 2: Menu Pages
- /menu (overview with all categories)
- /menu/signature-bowls (salmon, tuna, specialty)
- /menu/build-your-bowl (customization options)
- /menu/sushi-rolls
- /menu/boba-drinks
- /menu/sweets (mochi, desserts)

### Cluster 3: Transactional Pages
- /order (first-party ordering)
- /catering (group orders, events)
- /gift-cards

### Cluster 4: Blog Content (Months 3+)
- "What is Poke? A Guide to Hawaiian Bowls"
- "How to Build the Perfect Poke Bowl"
- "5 Reasons Poke is the Healthiest Lunch Option"
