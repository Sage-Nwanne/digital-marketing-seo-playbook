# 6-Month SEO Strategy: Moon Poke, Sushi & Sweets

**Client:** Moon Poke, Sushi & Sweets  
**Location:** East Stroudsburg, PA  
**Goal:** Improve organic visibility, migrate to first-party ordering, increase margins

---

## Strategic Objectives

| Objective | Target | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Rebuild SEO-optimized website | 20+ indexed pages | Month 1-2 |
| Migrate to first-party ordering | Eliminate 15-20% fees | Month 2-3 |
| Rank for primary keywords | Top 10 for 15+ keywords | Month 3-4 |
| Build customer database | 1,000+ contacts | Month 4-6 |
| Establish content rhythm | 2-4 posts/month | Month 4+ |
